Barter System App Application

In the age of the internet, everyone is connected and it is easy to share information with everyone. What if we could share our physical belongings just as easily as we share information! So we have created the barter system app.

Let's talk about the user stories -

1. Administer - the administer have to check who have signin and login in the app.

2. Donor - the donor is the one who will be donating the items that are being donated.

3. Requester - the requester is the one who will be rquesting the items and anybody interested will be donating him/her.
